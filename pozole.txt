Ingredients:
 - 1 gallon hominy corn
 - 1/2 onion, halved
 - Knorr sabor de pollo
 
Directions:
 1. Bring ~20 ounces of water to a boil.  You'll want to use enough to boil the onion.
 2. Cut an onion in half, then half the halves.  These quarters go into the boiling water.
 3. Let onion low boil for 15 minutes.
	a. In meantime, open gallon of hominy corn, drain in pasta strainer, and rinse of excess juice.
 4. Add ~2 tablespoons of Knorr to boiling water.  Let boil for 5 more minutes.
	a. Pour rinsed and drained hominy into heat-resistant container large enough to hold it.
 5. Remove knorr-onion water from heat and pour into container.
 6. Add water as desired to dilute knorr / to make soupier.
